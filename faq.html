FAQ

Your questions. Our answers!

Where/When Can I get tickets to Prom?

Check out the Bids tab to see both pricing and dates for which we will be selling tickets.

What are we going to be doing at Prom?

Prom is a once in a lifetime experience that will consist of an amazing dance on a boat, at the San Francisco pier. Plan on eating a yummy dinner, turning up with your homies, and dancing your hearts out! There will also be a photo booth to take pictures!

What do we do when we arrive to Lynbrook on prom day?

When you arrive, you will have a chance to get your prom photo’s taken by Pro Image, if you purchased a photo package. The photos will be taken in the Gym! Make sure to purchase your photo packages ahead of time so that you can skip the wait in line :) Other than that they’ll be a bunch of snacks and music! It’s a great time to get pumped and excited to get to prom!!

How are we getting to SF?

The only way that you can enter the boat at Senior Prom is by going with the busses that you will have signed up for on the Bus Form. If you’re not signed up for a bus, message a Class Officer, because that’s the only way that you’ll be allowed on the boat!

What should I bring to prom?

A swag tux/dress, some form of identification (best is school ID), flats (girls y’all will not be allowed to walk barefoot on the boat), some sick shades, and all your friends!

If I’m paying for my guest, do they need to fill out the other forms as well?

Yes, they need to fill out ALL forms as well.

What happens if my guest is not from Lynbrook?

Make sure to have them fill out all of the forms (Guest Form, Medical Form) and bring those along with a copy of their driver’s license to administration and have them sign it before purchasing a bid.

Can we change the seat on our bus after its been finalized to sit near a friend?

Everyone in 2017 is your friend! So no.

Can I return my Senior Prom ticket to someone else?

All sales are final.
