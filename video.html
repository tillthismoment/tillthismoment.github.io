The video dropped April 30th, at 9pm

Every Lynbrook takes a different path in high school, whether it's through dance, art, sports, or spirit. Watch as four of our most talented classmates explore their experiences Till This Moment, discovering their passions, creating bonds, and eventually go to prom.

This video is split into 3 sections, the introduction (the first day of freshman year), the journeys (each of the characters has their own video in which they explore their 4 years of high school, choose which journey you want to follow at the end of the intro video!), and the conclusion (their stories and talents merge in a finale)

Join the Lynbrook Class of 2017 on May 13th on the San Francisco Hornblower as we celebrate our achievements and experiences from these last four years.
